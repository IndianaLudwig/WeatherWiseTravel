Information about WeatherWise Travel

WeatherWise Travel is a web app developed by Travel Clear to aid in travel planning by providing recommendations based on where a person is leaving from, going to, the dates they are at the destination, the activities they want to enjoy, and the weather forecasted for while they are at the destination. WeatherWise Travel presents an all-in-one travel information hub providing information on flights, car rentals, hotels, the weather, and various activity locations at the travel destination along with a map. More information about the locations can be obtained simply by selecting them from the results. As for the weather categorization, WeatherWise Travel classifies the forecasted weather at the destination as follows:

General:

  • Pleasent - wind speed of 50 or below, no anticipated rain or snow, cloud cover of 40 or below, and a temperature between 85 and 32 degrees Farenheit inclusive.
  • Extremely Hot - A temperature above 85 degrees Farenheit and humidity above 70%.
  • Sunny - A temperature above 85 degrees Farenheit.
  • Cold - A temperature below 32 degrees Fahrenheit, or freezing.
  • Rainy - Rain being anticipated.
  • Snowy - Snow being anticipated.
  • Overcast - Cloud cover being greater than 70.
  • Partially Cloudy - Cloud cover between 41 and 70 inclusive.
  • Extermely Windy - A wind speed greater than 50.
  • Windy - A wind speed between 21 and 50 inclusive.
  • High UV Index - A UV index greater than 7, only considered in a few scenarios.

Exceptions:

  • Cold and Rainy - Rain being anticipated and both temperature below 50 degrees Fahrenheit and humidity above 70%.
  • Cold and Humid - A temperature below 32 degrees Farenheit, or freezing, and humidity above 70%.
  • Extremely Hot with Moderate UV Index - A temperature above 85 degrees Farenheit and UV index greater than 5.

Note that WeatherWise Travel will only recommend outdoor activities if the forecasted weather on the selected day is amicable to the outdoor activity chosen, otherwise presenting indoor activities as recommended.

Information about APIs used

WeatherWise Travel leverages information from the following API's to provide its users with valuable insights:

  • SkyScanner - Information on flights.
  • Meteomatics - Insights on weather.
  • Mapbox - Geocoding service utilized for map generation.
  • Google Places - Information on location, car rentals, and hotels.
